Shopping for clothes online used to be such a hastle. After browsing for hours and waiting for something to arrive, we'd get it in the mail only to find out how much better it looks on the model!

The Problem: Models aren't me, how does that look on my body?

I can give them my measurements, and show them what outfits I've loved in the past. But what would it take for online retailers to put me in their clothes?

The Solution: Image-In It - your first shopping avatar.

Image-In It sets up a virtual you. Just load a profile with your measurements and tastes, and Image-In It will integrate with your favorite online retailers (think JCrew, The Dressing Room, and StyleBOP). Now you won't have to imagine yourself in that shirt, you'll have a 3D view of how it fits right there on the screen.

But this is more than just some pretty pictures. Image-In It also lets you browse and rate outfits from the same designers, recommends new outfits based on your past purchases, and even sends you personalized coupons when you're ready to buy!

FAQ

How does Image-In It work?
Image-In It saves your information in a secure personal profile. We combine that profile with clothing data provided by vendors to create a 3D view of how you might look in those clothes.
How do you make such great outfits for me?
Image-In It uses the same advanced algorithms as Netflix and Amazon do when they recommend a movie or a new gadget. Along with data from online retailers and some image processing, we're starting to get pretty good at it!
